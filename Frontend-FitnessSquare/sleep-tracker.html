Sleep Better Tips

  • Consistency: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day to regulate your body clock.
  • Digital Detox: Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bed to prevent blue light disruption.
  • Environment: Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet for optimal sleep cycles.
  • Dietary Habits: Limit caffeine and heavy meals in the late afternoon and evening.
