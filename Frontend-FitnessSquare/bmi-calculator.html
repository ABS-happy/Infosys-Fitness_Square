What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measure using height and weight to categorize adults as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
BMI is a screening tool and doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.
